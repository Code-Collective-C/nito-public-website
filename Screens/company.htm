About us

Nito Energy (Pty) Ltd is a wholesaler of fuel distribution and we offer our service to clients throughout Southern Africa. We pride ourselves in 8 years of efficient, reliable service and a proficiency at what we are offering.

Our dedication to provide a reliable service to our clients is of the highest priority and we therefore strive to satisfy all our clients’ needs by working efficiently and effectively.

Nito Energy (previously known as “Kelrn Bulk Energy”) is situated in Kimberley, South Africa and forms part of the Renati Group (Pty) Ltd.
